Privacy Policy

In order to protect your privacy, IC2 Planner does NOT
collect personal data or transmit them, not even in
anonymized form, to anyone.

Nevertheless, IC2 Planner is NOT able to automatically or manually
retrieve information about user device from the third
party internet-based services.

Consequently, IC2 Planner is using camera and device
storage only to store and process
reactor schemes locally. Users can share schemes via other social
 applications and can fully control sharing data.
