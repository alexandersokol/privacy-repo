Privacy Policy

In order to protect your privacy, IC2 Planner does NOT
collect personal data or transmit them, not even in
anonymized form, to anyone.

Nevertheless, IC2 Planner is able to automatically
retrieve information about user device from third
party internet-base services.

Consequently, IC2 Planner is using camera and device
storage only to store and process
reactor schemes locally. User can share schemes via 
other social application and can fully control sharing data.
